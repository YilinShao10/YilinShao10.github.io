Music Emotion Computing


Introduction

Music Emotion Computing (MEC) is an interdisciplinary research field that focuses on analyzing and predicting emotions conveyed by music. It combines elements from music information retrieval (MIR), affective computing, machine learning, psychology, and neuroscience. The ultimate goal of MEC is to enhance human-computer interaction, recommendation systems, and music therapy applications by enabling machines to understand and generate music with emotional relevance.
