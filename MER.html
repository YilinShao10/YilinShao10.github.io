Emotion-Driven Audio Interaction


Background

During my time in a music emotion computing group in the NEXTLAB at Zhejiang University, I contributed as a designer by conceptualizing product functionalities and creating low-fidelity prototypes. This team explored how to harness emotion analysis to automatically generate or select background music for videos. Later, this technology was successfully integrated into a mini-program for the Hangzhou Asian Games, with many of the features I proposed being implemented in the final product.
