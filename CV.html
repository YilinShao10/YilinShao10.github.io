CV

My name is Yilin Shao, and I am currently a third-year graduate student in Design Science at Zhejiang University. My research interests lie in the fields of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), tangible interfaces, and sensory design. I aim to explore how emerging technologies can transform the way we perceive and engage with the world, fostering more intuitive and meaningful interactions between humans and their environments.
